Logomark & clear space
The crate is a parts bin — its four compartments are the colour system and spell OSMM in pixel type. The lettered mark stands alone (icons, avatars, favicons); pair the plain crate with the wordmark in lockups. Keep clear space of one bin-height on all sides.

Usage
✓ Do
  • Keep the four bin colours in O-S-M-M order.
  • Use white mark on dark, charcoal mark on light.
  • Give it room — one bin of clear space.
✕ Don't
  • Recolour the crate outline or stretch it.
  • Add shadows, gradients or outlines to the bins.
  • Place the dark mark on a busy or low-contrast field.
